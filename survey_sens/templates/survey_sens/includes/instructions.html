Two participants take part in this study: you and another randomly chosen participant.
Each participant will receive one of two roles: Sender or Receiver.
The roles are determined randomly. {% if session.config.info %} You will find out what role you got as soon as you answer the short questionnaire. {% endif %}
Depending on the role, you can either change the bonus that you and the other participant will get or not.
The study proceeds as follows:
  1. You will first fill in a short questionnaire.
  2. Then you will be randomly assigned to one of two roles (Sender or Receiver)
  3. The participant who receives the role of Sender will make a decision that affects the bonus of both participants.
Decision:
The Sender has the option to split $ 1 (100 cents) between the Receiver and himself. The Sender decides how much of the amount he sends to the Receiver. The rest of the amount remains for the Sender.
The part of 100 cents that the Sender decided to send to the Recipient will be paid to the Recipient in the form of a bonus.
The remaining part of 100 cents will be paid to the Sender as a bonus.

Thus, the Sender can affect the bonus received in Toloka by both participants, but the Recipient cannot.
